Clicker Game Rubric

For the next 2 weeks, you will be working on your clicker game

Requirements:

  • HTML, CSS, and at least 2 Javascript files
  • At least 150 lines of Javascript
  • The main game component is mouse inputs (left click)
  • Use an array, a structure, 4 global variables, and export/import
  • Use Math family functions, such as round, floor, random, etc
  • Use a flexbox or a grid
  • A minimum of 5 functions and 2 event listeners

Hints:

  • Need ideas? Look at old clicker games such as Clicker Heroes or Tap Titans.
  • Stuck, lost, or confused? Look at old files we've created and then ask for help.
  • Want to include something we have not learned? Google is your best friend.
  • First have an idea, plan, then start coding.

YOU HAVE 4 HOURS REMAINING (4 class hours)
